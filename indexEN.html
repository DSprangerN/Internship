What is our Mission?

At A Estrelinha Amarela Kindergarten, our mission is to continue family and social values and provide harmonious development for children. In this sense, the safety, well-being and happiness of our children are our absolute priority. By respecting each child's individual pace, we aim to enhance their development in a natural and balanced way.

Our guiding principles are simple and clear:

  • Stimulate the development of autonomy;
  • Promote an “open door” culture, where parents are welcome to participate in proposed activities;
  • Encourage experiences that support growth and learning;
  • Support children's self-recognition;
  • Promote positive and integrated relationship with others;
  • Contribute to emotional stability and security while respecting each children's individuality;
  • Encourage respect for the family, their cultural identities and the values of all cultures;
  • Ensure the acquisition of knowledge about nature, society and culture;
  • Promote physical development through activities that are appropriate for each age group;
  • Ensure that everyone is treated equally.
