Staff

Márcia Vicente

Márcia Vicente has a solid background in Management and as Technical Director ensures the smooth operation of the institution, promoting a safe and harmonious environment focused on children's needs and their families.

She is responsible for technical and administrative coordination, ensuring compliance with legal and pedagogical requirements as well as working closely with the educational team, families and partner organizations.

Bruna Vicente

Bruna Vicente is a passionate educator with a warm and inspiring presence, leaving a positive mark on everyone she works and learns with. With specialized training in Early Childhood Education, Bruna brings over 15 years of experience dedicated to childhood and the holistic development of children.

As Pedagogical Director, she coordinates educational practices with sensitivity, vision, and rigor, fostering an environment where each child is valued as unique and encouraged to grow with joy, autonomy, and confidence. Bruna believes in the transformative power of education and invests daily in building positive relationships among the team, children, and families, as well as in her own professional development.

Filipa Macedo

Filipa Macedo has been part of the Estrelinha Amarela team since early 2019 and since then has been a constant presence of care, dedication, and responsibility. As an educational assistant, Filipa accompanies the children’s daily routines with attention, affection and great sensitivity to their individual needs.

With a collaborative and cheerful spirit, Filipa supports daily activities with joy and commitment, contributing to a safe, calm and nurturing environment. Her close relationship with children reflects the care and respect she brings to everything she does — from hygiene and meals to playtime, outings and daily interactions.

Filipa is also a colleague who is always ready to help, with an easy smile and a generous heart, becoming a trusted figure for families, the team, and the children.
