AT A Estrelinha Amarela Kindergarten, we prioritize the holistic development of each child, offering a diverse range of curricular and extracurricular activities designed to enrich their educational journey through playful, creative, and stimulating experiences.



Curricular Activities

Included in the monthly fee, our carefully selected curricular activities support the physical, emotional, cognitive, and social development of children. These activities take place once a week, lasting 45 minutes each, and are held exclusively during the school term, being suspended during school holidays. In the current school year, we offer:

  • English - A playful introduction to the English language, encouraging early contact with a second language.
  • Motor Expression - Physical activities that develop motor coordination, balance, and body awareness.
  • Musical Expression - Exploration of sounds, rhythms, and instruments, fostering creativity and an appreciation for music.




Extracurricular Activities


In addition, we offer optional extracurricular activities tailored to the interests and motivations of the children. This selection is reviewed annually, taking into account the preferences expressed by our little ones and their families. We believe that through these diverse experiences, we contribute to richer, more meaningful, and joyful learning.

For the current school year, we offer:

  • Dance - Provides children with a fun and creative way to express themselves through movement. To the sound of various musical styles, children develop motor coordination, rhythmic sense, body awareness, and self-confidence, while also promoting teamwork, concentration, and discipline. During the classes, children explore simple choreographies, movement games, and body expression exercises.
