Privacy Policy

At Estrelinha Amarela Kindergarten is committed to protecting the privacy of its users, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Which data do we collect?

We only collect the personal data necessary for school and administrative management, namely: First name, Last name, username, check-in and check-out times, and contact details provided in the registration forms.

2. For what purposes do we use the data?

The data is used exclusively for:

  • Internal management of staff and time records;
  • Communication with guardians and staff members;
  • Management of registrations and school activities.

3. Who has access to the data?

Access to the data is restricted to the management and staff members authorized by the management. Data is not shared with third parties without consent, except when required by law.

4. How long do we keep the data?

Data is retained only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and in accordance with legal obligations.

5. Data subjects' rights

Data subjects may, at any time, request access, rectification, updating, or deletion of their personal data by contacting the data controller.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.
